Rainbow lager (copper beer)

The Rainbow lager is a classic student beer you can see these in every student event. The name copper beer comes from the cans color which is copper like orange.

The price of copper beer is around 0,99€ the price can be different depending of the shop you buy it from, Prisma and S-markets are cheaper than Alepa. The taste is good but a bit watery so the rating we gave is 4.6/5!

Pirkka III-beer (Sausage water)

Pirkka III-beer is also very easy to spot in student events because it's blue color. We name the beer nakkivesi (sausage water) because of it's taste.

Price of the Pirkka III-beer is around 1,15€ so it's the most expensive one of the cheap beers. The taste is also not that great, but some times you are in a hurry and the clock is getting close to 9 pm, so you get what ever you can get your hands on! We gave a rating of 2.8/5 because it's taste is not that great and it's by far the most expensive one.
